Recognize your current position(认清目前的位置)  
Clarify your future goals(明确未来的目标)  
and establish a clear exit strategy(以及设定好退路)  
A positive-yielding, straightforward method(一个正收益，简单方法)  
repeated a hundred or a thousand times(重复一百遍一千遍)  
can create miracles(就能创造奇迹)  

--The Essence of Trading(交易的本质)
